By some seemingly small string spun

By the silent hands of the Fates fancy,

Led to a significant chance —

Of meeting you as Spring is to Hades’ eternity

And as we forge forward in fear and faith,

Fondness was found where Fates once forbade.

And all the phantoms of foregone past,

Fade as we follow the fire we’ve fanned.

With light on land where our love last lay,

A life well-lived and a life well-loved.

In wellness and death, no matter how long

Forever longing — waiting, waiting.
